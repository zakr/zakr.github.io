ABOUT ME

My name is Zak Rome.

I have a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Arizona's Honors College. Before that, I was an IB student at Desert Mountain High School in Scottsdale, Arizona. My honors thesis was "Utilizing the GAP Programming Language to Study Computational Abstract Algebra." I've used Java, C, Javascript, HTML, Haskell, Ruby, Prolog, and GAP between my academic and work careers.

Beyond all that I enjoy playing music, reading, watching films, and writing.
